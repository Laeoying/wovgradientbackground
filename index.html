Make your own theme !
Themes are linear diagonal color gradients, from the first color at the top left, through all the others in order, to the last color at the bottom right.
To create your theme, simply choose colors in the order you wish, based on the hexadecimal color code.
You can choose from 2 to 7 colors.
Choose the color you like from the site shown below after clicking "picker" and then copy the 6 characters next to the # to place them in the text fields.
Click Save when you're done.
